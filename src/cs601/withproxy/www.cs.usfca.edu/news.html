CS Night - December 5th 6:30pm

posted Nov 7, 2013, 3:50 PM by Sami Rollins   [ updated Nov 13, 2013, 6:36 PM ]

Our 12th Annual Computer Science Night celebration will be Thursday, December 5, 2013.  

Please RSVP on the CS Night webpage !

This year we are excited to introduce three new tenure-track faculty members and show off our stellar new classrooms in the LCSI.  This year's events will also feature the 2nd Annual Game Jam Hackathon for students and alums and, as always, impressive student posters and demonstrations, great food and drink, and a chance to see old friends and professors. We'll have a short "State of the Department" address, and we hope you'll have plenty of time to explore our new facilities.

Schedule

4:00 pm: 2nd Annual Game Jam Hackathon in LCSI G12. Everyone is invited!

6:30 pm: Posters and Demonstrations of students work, food and drink, LCSI 307 and breakout area

7:30 pm: State of the Department Address featuring introductions of new faculty Sophie Engle, Alark Joshi, and Cindi Thompson

Event parking will be available in the Koret lot.

USF launches new MS in Analytics program

posted Mar 1, 2012, 10:59 AM by Terence Parr

This is a new multidisciplinary MS analytics graduate program offered jointly by the College of Arts and Sciences and the School of Management. It focuses on the mathematical, statistical, and computational techniques needed to make the data driven decisions that are central to effective business strategies. The program covers techniques and approaches from statistics, applied mathematics, data mining and machine learning, econometrics and risk analysis, and operations research.

Sriram Sankar speaking at USF CS department

posted Nov 28, 2011, 3:22 PM by Terence Parr

Sriram Sankar will be speaking at my web systems and algorithms course on Wednesday, November 30 at 2:15pm in HR232 (the big auditorium). His title is "Real-World Experiences with Search Engines".
